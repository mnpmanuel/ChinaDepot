FAQ

  • Do we need to stay in Lisbon after the program?
    No. But, we do include another 3 months of free office and maker space access if you do want to stay. Besides, Lisbon is the fastest growing startup ecosystem in Europe, why leave?
  • We are from a galaxy far, far away. Can we still apply?
    Yes. If the place you currently call home is outside the EU you will need a Schengen VISA. It’s easy to attain for 90 days and we will help you get them once your company gets accepted.
  • What kind of products are you most interested?
    The ones you can touch and feel. You have as much chance of being accepted with a dumb wooden chair or a satellite. What we most value is user experience… we want to help make products, not objects!
  • Will you sign an NDA before we apply?
    No. We don’t ask for anything that could compromise your product innovations in the application process.
  • Can you help me protect my IP?
    Yes. During the acceleration program you will have access to patent services.
  • We have been working on X for a while and already have Y. Can we still apply? Can you still help us?
    Yes, we consider applications in any stage. Even if you are already in the market and/or raised a significant amount of money we can still leverage everything you accomplished and help you scale.
  • Are you looking for help?
    Sort of. We have a solid team in Lisbon and it is unlikely we need to hire anyone else until we decide to expand to other cities. That being said, our portfolio companies are always hiring and we do have internships opportunities.
  • What is No Bullshit Lab?
    No Bullshit Lab is an acceleration program that gives startups, crafters and makers the resources to create sustainable products. Up to 10 companies at a time will be given the tools and connections to build and bring their products to market.
  • What is an accelerator?
    A startup accelerator is a fixed-term, cohort-based program, that includes mentorship and educational components normally culminating in a public pitch event or demo day.
  • What will I do and learn during the program?
    We work on a case by case basis to establish a plan that take into account you, your product and your market. We won’t make you attend workshops about your area of expertise if that is what you’re asking… but it could be useful for someone else in your team to attend. A startup is all about everyone helping in everything.
  • What can we expect by the end of the program?
    By the end of the program you can be selecting a factory, launching a crowdfunding campaign, meeting with investors to raise a funding round or already in a retail channel. Keep in mind that launching a physical product takes a lot of time, even if your already at an MVP the average time to market is still around 9 months. We accelerate, we do no magic.
  • Why should I give you up to 10% of my startup?
    If you try adding all our perks together, you will easily reach a value that justifies what we ask. However, as entrepreneurs we will tell you that most value will come from having a group of people that have tried, failed and succeeded by your side during and after the program for as long as you keep trying.
  • Why 10%?
    By asking for 10% we are valuing your startup at 250k€ since 25k€ is our average cost per startup in the program. If you feel your startup is worth more just tell us why, we will be happy to consider your arguments.
  • Do we need to have a legally established company before we apply?
    No, but you will eventually need to open one. We will help you set one up if needed.
  • Do we need to move to Lisbon for the duration of the program?
    Yes, at least two of you need to locally attend the program. We strongly suggest you have your entire team here for the duration of the program, it really accelerates everything.
